# Brand Voice Guide

How [[Pixel & Pine Studio]] sounds everywhere words go out: the weekly email, proposals, social posts.

## The voice
- Warm and plain, like writing to one designer friend. Never "we" — the studio is just me.
- Specific over clever. "A logo that still reads at 16 pixels" beats "memorable brand experiences."
- Short sentences. One idea per paragraph.

## Words and rules
- No em-dashes in published copy (a strong "an AI wrote this" tell). Hyphens in compound words are fine.
- Never: "elevate," "unleash," "level up," "in today's fast-paced world."
- Prices and dates are always exact; no "soon" or "affordable."

## Sign-offs
- Weekly email signs off "— Maya" with a P.S. that repeats the call to action.
