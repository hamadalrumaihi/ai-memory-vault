# SAHP

San Andreas Highway Patrol — my department. One subfolder per subdivision, plus the Jobs that handle my paperwork.

## Subfolders
- `CID/` — criminal investigations: cases, gang files, my method. See [[CID]].
- `FTO/` — field training: trainees and evaluations. See [[FTO]].
- `Air 1/` — aviation unit ops. See [[Air 1]].
- `Jobs/` — recurring paperwork the AI does my way:
  - [[Write the Incident Report]] — official report from a callout or arrest.
  - [[Build the Case File]] — open and grow a CID case note from whatever thread starts it.
  - [[Write the Trainee Evaluation]] — FTO phase evals in department format.
